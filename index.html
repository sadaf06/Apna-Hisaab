Apna Hisaab Mein Kya Kya Hai

✍️

Aaj Screen

Roz ka home — ek line likho ya bolo, AI turant expense, saving, ya goal-update samajh ke add kar deta hai. Streak badge bhi dikhta hai.

🗣️

Natural Hinglish Parsing

"100 ki chai pi", "6000 rent diya" — jaise bolte ho waise likho. Gemini AI amount, category, item sab khud nikalta hai.

🎯

Multi-Intent Detection

Ek hi sentence mein multiple actions: "100 ki chai pi aur 500 bachaye" → expense bhi, saving bhi — dono alag se log honge.

🎙️

Voice Input

Type karne ka mann nahi? Mic dabao aur seedhe bolke entry daalo — hands-free hisaab.

😊

Mood Tracking

Har entry ke saath apna mood bhi capture hota hai — Khush, Normal, Thaka, Stressed, Sad. Patterns time ke saath dikhte hain.

📖

Month ki Kahani

Mahine ke end mein AI likhta hai tumhari apni kahani — spending aur mood dono ko milake ek warm, personal Hinglish summary.

🎯

Sapna — Savings Goals

Apne sapne set karo (Goa trip, naya phone), paisa bachao, aur progress ring se track karo — poora hone pe confetti celebration!

📊

Category Limits

Har category (Khana, Safar, Masti, Shopping) ka apna monthly budget target set karo — limit cross hone pe pata chal jaayega.

💰

Income Sources

Salary, freelance, ya koi bhi income source track karo — alag se manage karo apni kamaai.

🔒

Privacy PIN Lock

Kamaai kisi se chhupani hai? 4-digit PIN se income visibility lock kar do, ek tap mein unlock.

🔔

Daily Reminders

Raat 9 baje gentle nudge notification, taaki hisaab likhna kabhi na chuke.

🌙

Liquid Glass UI

Modern glassmorphic dark theme, floating navbar pill, sheen aur blur effects — premium feel har screen pe.

🔐

Secure Auth & Sync

Firebase Auth se login, Firestore se data sync — har device pe tumhara hisaab safe aur up-to-date.

⚙️

Settings & Control

Naam edit, password reset, logout, account delete — sab kuch ek jagah, apni marzi se control karo.

🎉

Celebration Moments

Goal poora karne pe confetti burst — chhoti jeet ko bhi celebrate karne ka mauka.
